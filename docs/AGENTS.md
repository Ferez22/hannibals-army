# Agents

## Overview of main agents

The system is composed of multiple agents that work together to provide a comprehensive digital twin experience.

### The 'DONNA'

DONNA is the agent that makes sure your digital twin is up to date and accurate. It decides if a plan suits you or not, by analyzing your digital twin and the plan.

### The 'HANNIBAL'

HANNIBAL plans your life: it packages tasks and generates action items. It comes up with long term strategies and short term plans.

### The '007'

007 is the agent that is with you 24/7, providing real-time assistance with your daily activities, tasks, does research to answer questions.

## Other agents

### The 'RAGNAR'

RAGNAR is the agent that manages the retrieval of information from the internet and other sources. He goes to war with his crew, gathers information, and brings t back to HANNIBAL..
